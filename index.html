About Akotarh

A Life at the Intersection of
Technology & Impact

Akotarh Akoson, formerly known publicly as Raymond Akoson / Akoson Raymond, is a Senior Cybersecurity Engineer, civic-tech leader, former politician, activist, and founder. His work sits at the intersection of cybersecurity, education, institutional management, civic advocacy, and secure digital transformation.

"Secure Systems. Strong Institutions. Empowered Communities — this is not a tagline. It is a life's work."

From teaching Biology in Cameroon to serving as Secretary General of a political party, from parliamentary candidacy to a Magna Cum Laude cybersecurity degree — every chapter has sharpened the same mission.
