Official Statement on False Online Claims

I am aware that false and harmful claims have circulated online misusing my name and image.

These claims are completely false. I have no involvement in the activities suggested by those posts, and I reject the misuse of my identity in that context.

My public and professional work remains focused on cybersecurity, technology, education, civic leadership, human-rights advocacy, and secure digital transformation.

I continue to document and address harmful misuse of my name and image through appropriate channels.

Akotarh Akoson
