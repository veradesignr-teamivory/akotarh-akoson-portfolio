Cybersecurity · IT Services

America CyberSquad

Enterprise-grade cybersecurity and IT services made accessible to the organizations mainstream providers overlook — small businesses, nonprofits, churches, civic institutions, and community organizations.

"Every large breach I've investigated had one thing in common: it started with a smaller, under-resourced partner. Small organizations are not low-value targets — they are low-friction entry points into larger networks. That's why protecting them matters."

— Akotarh Akoson, Founder
Security Assessments & Audits

Comprehensive vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and security posture reviews for organizations of all sizes.

Managed IT & Security Services

Ongoing managed security and IT support — monitoring, threat response, patching, and infrastructure management.

Security Awareness Training

Human-centered security training programs, phishing simulations, and policy development for non-technical staff.

Secure Digital Transformation

Guiding organizations through cloud migration, tool modernization, and digital infrastructure upgrades with security built in from day one.

Venture 02
EdTech · Platform

Scholarstika

An education management platform providing structured tools for managing scholarships, academic programs, student records, and institutional workflows — built for schools, foundations, and educational nonprofits navigating complex administrative demands.

"I have navigated educational systems on two continents — some that worked and many that didn't. The difference was almost always administrative infrastructure. Scholarstika exists to give institutions the backend they need to deliver on their educational mission."

— Akotarh Akoson, Founder
Scholarship Management

End-to-end scholarship lifecycle management: applications, reviews, award tracking, disbursements, and reporting.

Academic Program Administration

Tools for managing courses, curricula, enrollment, attendance, and academic progress across programs and cohorts.

Student Records & Compliance

Secure student record management with compliance controls, audit trails, and data governance features.

Faith-Tech · Community

Mandem

A church management platform providing faith communities with a comprehensive digital backbone for member management, event coordination, giving tracking, communications, and operational administration.

"Churches are among the most trusted institutions in many communities — and among the most administratively under-resourced. Mandem gives them the tools to serve their congregations with the same operational clarity that any well-run organization would have."

— Akotarh Akoson, Founder
Member Management

Comprehensive member profiles, family connections, attendance tracking, and engagement history in one place.

Giving & Finance Tracking

Donation tracking, pledge management, tithing records, and financial reporting with full audit trails.

Events & Communications
